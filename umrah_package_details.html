Hotel Policies

You must present a photo ID when checking in. Room types such as bed & smoking preferences are subject to availability. Your reservation is prepaid, and the hotel will not request payment from you at the check in. The total paid charges include transportation to the hotel, hotel check in and hotel taxes. Any incidental charges such as parking, phone calls, and room services will be handled directly between the guest and the property. Please note that certain municipalities in some cities also levy local taxes on the service provided. These taxes cannot be collected in advance and must be borne directly by the customers and should be paid during the checkout from the hotel.
For your information, local laws may restrict unmarried guests from sharing room. Couples are required to provide a proof of marriage if they wish to share a room. Alcohol is not served or permitted on any property in Saudi Arabia.

Description

Hotel is located in Medina, just 700 metres from The Prophet's Mosque. Free Wi-Fi access is available. Featuring a city view, air-conditioned rooms here come with a seating area, flat-screen satellite TV and fridge. At Nibras Al-Shahbaa you will find a 24-hour front desk and a mini-market on site. Other facilities offered include shops (on site). The hotel is 2.2 km from Madina Mall and 3.7 km from Al Noor Mall.
