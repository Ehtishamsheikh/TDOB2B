About Us

Travel Destination Online a complete B2B travel Management Company specialized in providing excellent travel related services and products such as hotels, furnished service apartments, transfers, professionally guided sightseeing tours and packages offered to its customers around the world.

We are incomparable when it comes to customer service and cost-containment. We believe that the innovation is vital to our success; therefore, flexible service options and our online booking engine will keep our business relations on the leading edge.

Our winning situation means that we will secure the best possible value for your business without compromising standard of quality, service or convenience. Travel Destination Online committed to placing the interests of our customers at priority. We consider this key element as one of our primary objective to establish relationship with every individual based on transparency, mutual business understanding and deliver our commitment with dedication.

Overview

Travel Destination Online has taken a step ahead by developing its own Online Booking Engine that caters to the fast growing demand of online bookings market. Our team of professionals range from excellent and proven track records in the Tourism industry. We specialize in Free Independent Travelers (FIT) & (MICE) Meetings, Incentives, Conventions and Exhibitions and have been actively and successfully handling both segments from Middle East and Asia.

Our clients include from SUBC, Middle East and GCC. We are also delighted to share that the success and strength in FIT & MICE (Groups) has become the symbol of our company and what we are now renowned for. In return it has made us into one of the premier Company in the Region.

The reputation that we have developed has further encouraged us into focusing and creating the awareness of FIT importance amongst our travel partners. With this goal in mind we have gone from strength to strength in negotiating the most competitive rates with Hotels and Suppliers in accordance to the demands of our specific markets. In developing and maintaining a cost-effective travel policy for your company, we will not only make sure that you get to your destination, but our destination management and caretaker team will work with you to ensure that your journey is seamless.

Vision
